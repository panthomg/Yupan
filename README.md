**For Desktop Users:**
1. Navigate to [**Yupan**](https://tintool.netlify.app/yupan2mobile/).

Yupan is highly optimized for touch devices. For the best full-screen experience:
1. Open the [**Yupan link**](https://tintool.netlify.app/yupan2mobile/) in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android).
2. Tap the **Share** button (iOS) or **Menu** button (Android).
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Launch Yupan directly from your home screen like a native app!

## ğŸ› ï¸ How to Use Yupan (User Guide)

### 1. Navigating the Canvas
*   **Pan:** Click and drag anywhere on the empty background. On mobile, use a single finger.
*   **Zoom:** Use your mouse scroll wheel, or pinch with two fingers on a mobile device.

### 2. Creating and Editing Nodes
*   **Add Idea:** Click the **"+ Node"** button to drop a new block onto the center of your screen. Click inside it to start typing.
*   **Move Idea:** Click and drag the striped handle at the top of a box node (or drag anywhere on an oval node) to move it.
*   **Customize Idea:** **Right-click** (or **Long-press** on mobile) on any node to open the Context Menu. Here you can change its color, shape, duplicate it, or delete it.

### 3. Connecting Ideas
*   Click the **"ğŸ”— Connect"** button. Your cursor will turn into a crosshair.
*   Click the **first node**, then click the **second node**. A line will automatically link them!
*   *To delete a connection:* Simply click directly on the connecting line.

### 4. Customizing Your Layout
Don't like where the buttons are? Change them!
*   Click **"ğŸ¨ Customize"** and go to the **"Layout"** tab.
*   You will enter the Layout Editor. Click and drag the UI buttons to wherever you want them. Use the drag handles to resize them.
*   Click **"Save Layout"** at the top right.

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I share my board with someone else?**  
A: First, ensure you are friends on Yupan by searching for their username in the "ğŸ§‘â€ğŸ¤â€ğŸ§‘ Friends" menu. Once they accept your friend request, go to your dashboard, click "Share" on a board, and assign them "Viewer" or "Editor" permissions.

**Q: How do I unlock premium palettes (Vintage Grove, Synthwave, Solarized)?**  
A: Premium palettes unlock automatically once you connect with **5 friends** on Yupan! (Alternatively, you can use a promotional redeem code in your Profile menu).

**Q: The app isn't syncing my changes. What should I do?**  
A: Check the status indicator at the top of your screen. Yupan auto-saves 1.5 seconds after you stop typing or moving a node. If you see a "Connection Error", check your internet connection and refresh the page.

**Q: I broke my layout and the buttons are gone!**  
A: Go to `Customize > Layout` and click the **"Reset"** button to restore the UI to its factory default positions.
